Paid Time Off

You work hard. You deserve a break. Signature Flight Support helps team members relax and recharge by offering paid time away to celebrate holidays, take a vacation, be with family, or just catch up on life.

401K Retirement Plan

Save today and enjoy tomorrow! Signature Flight Support team members are super savers who take advantage of our 401(k) retirement savings program. Part-time team members may contribute pretax earnings to the fund on the first day of the first quarter following completion of 12 months of employment.

Full-time team members may contribute pre and post-tax contributions to the fund following the successful completion of the 90-day introductory period. Full-time team members hired January 1, 2017 or later will be eligible to receive the company's matching contribution one year after employment.

Tuition Reimbursement

Signature Flight Support values education and encourages continuous learning. The Company offers up to 85% tuition reimbursement for undergraduate studies, and up to 50% for graduate work.

Training & Development

In addition to providing comprehensive on-the-job training, Signature Flight Support recognizes the value great leaders bring to an organization. We offer professional development seminars and advanced leadership training in areas like effective communication, team building, coaching, diversity and inclusion, and more. Our Manager in Training (MIT) program offers a "fast-track" curriculum, designed to propel participants to upper level management positions.

Work-Life Balance

At Signature Flight Support, people come first. From your first day on the team, you'll have access to a complimentary Employee Assistance Program (EAP). This free confidential service is designed to help you and your family manage life. Team members use the dedicated website and phone number to access resources on topics like financial planning, stress management, and balancing work and life.

We work hard and have fun. Out teams enjoy fun team member engagement events like holiday decorating competitions and potluck lunches. We appreciate our loyal team members by celebrating milestone years of service and reward those who go Above and Beyond through our team member recognition program.

Picking out our clothes for work gets a whole lot easier with Signature Flight Support! We provide comfortable uniforms for all non-managerial positions.

Additional benefits may be available based on location. The Company will provide full details of benefits available during the application and interview process.

Registered Retirement Savings Plan (RRSP)

Save today and enjoy tomorrow! Signature Flight Support team members are super savers who take advantage of Registered Retirement Savings Plan (RRSP). The Plan allows team members to contribute pre-tax dollars into a target-date fund that matures closest to the year in which you reach age 65. Team members can make contributions between 1% and 6% of their earnings and are eligible to participate after 12 months of continuous service with the Company.
